Havard Cassandra

Havard Cassandra is French, working as an accountant, with a passion for reading English poetry. She sometimes writes poetry in her spare time. After gaining skills from several written poems, she decided to submit a poem to you. She mainly read classical literature. Her favorite poets are Percy Bysshe Shelley, Alfred Lord Tennyson, Robert Browning, John Keats. She mainly likes it, when the poem tells a story, while mixing rhythms and literary English.
