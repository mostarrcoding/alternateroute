Tre Luna

Tre Luna writes fiction and non-fiction. He has had horror shorts accepted by The Chamber Magazine (May 2022,) Dark Horses Magazine (February 2022,) and the anthology "Nightmare Fuel" by the Sci-Fi & Fantasy Writers Guild in partnership with Cloaked Press (October 2021.) His flash-fiction story was accepted by Idle Ink for publication in January 2022, and in December 2021 his poem was published by The Spotlong Review. He blogs about writing, being transgender and autistic, and the fabric arts, which can be found at https://panfae.medium.com, and his Twitter handle is @TreLuna5.
