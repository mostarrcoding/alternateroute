Abigail Eckstine

Abigail Eckstine (They/She) is a 25-year-old queer writer of novels and poetry. Most recently they have published a poetry collection titled Maybe (Not) Her, which explores themes of bisexuality and polyamory. Other works by Abigail have been published in Moonchild Magazine and Twist In Time. You can find them on twitter @whimsywriter3
