Jody Rae

Jody Rae's work appears in MASKS Literary Magazine, Sledgehammer Lit, The Babel Tower Notice Board, The Good Life Review, and Red Fez. Her short story, "Beautiful Mother" was a finalist in the Phoebe Journal 2021 Spring Fiction Contest. She has pieces forthcoming in Rejection Letters, RESURRECTION magazine, and XRAY. Her work can be found at www.criminysakesalive.com.
