Afra Ahmad

Afra Ahmad is a writer, poet, artist holding a great passion for Arabic calligraphy. Based in Saudi Arabia, she is pursuing bachelors in English Literature. She writes about everything under the sun: from dark issues of the society to problems faced by teenagers to imparting chunks of wisdom through her poems, stories and write-ups. Her works have been published in various magazines including Her Hearth, Melbourne Culture Corner, Iman collective.
