Michael Starr

Michael Starr is a former biologist who has been writing poetry recretionally since 2004. He is owner of a pseudonymous blog on WordPress of poetry and has been published for individual poems in BlazVOX, Aberration Labyrinth, Lipstick Party, Anapest, The Paragon Journal, etc. His first book of poetry, The Passage is Still, is available on Amazon.com, Kindle, and Nook. He lives in the Bay Area, California, with his family.
