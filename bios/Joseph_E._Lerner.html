Joseph E. Lerner

Joseph Lerner's fiction and poetry have appeared in 100 WordStory, BlazeVOX, decomP, fictive dream, Gargoyle, matchbook, Mojave River Review, and elsewhere. In 1981 he founded, as editor/publisher, THE WASHINGTON (DC) BOOK REVIEW and, in 1992, FURIOUS FICTIONS, A MAGAZINE OF SHORT-SHORT STORIES, one of the first literary journals devoted exclusively to flash fiction. Joseph Lerner currently lives in Frostburg, MD. His twitter handle is @je_lerner.
