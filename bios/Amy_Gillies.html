Amy Gillies

Amy Gillies is a contemporary artist who lives and works in London. In 2019, Amy completed an MFA in Fine Art from Kingston School of Art, where she had studied a BA Hons in Fine Art and Art History previously. Since graduating, Amy has taken part in the Artists in Residence programme at One Paved Court in Richmond and has exhibited and performed in several group exhibitions including The London Art Grads Now 2021 at Saatchi Gallery. Her poetry has been published in Secret Chords: A Poetry Anthology of the Best of Folklore Prize.
