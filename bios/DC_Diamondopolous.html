DC Diamondopolous

DC Diamondopolous is an award-winning short story, and flash fiction writer with over 300 stories published internationally in print and online magazines, literary journals, and anthologies. DC's stories have appeared in: Penmen Review, Progenitor, 34th Parallel, So It Goes: The Literary Journal of the Kurt Vonnegut Museum and Library, Lunch Ticket, and others. DC's short story collection Stepping Up includes Pushcart Prize and Sundress Publications' Best of the Net nominated stories. She lives on the California central coast with her wife and animals.
