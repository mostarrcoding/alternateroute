Frank William Finney

Frank William Finney is a retired lecturer currently based in Massachusetts. He lived in Thailand from 1995 until 2020, where he taught at Thammasat University. His poems have appeared in The Disappointed Housewife, Grand Little Things, Stone Poetry Journal, and The Thieving Magpie, among others. His chapbook The Folding of the Wings is forthcoming from Finishing Line Press in 2022.
