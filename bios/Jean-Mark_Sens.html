Jean-Mark Sens

Born in France, Jean-Mark Sens has lived in the American South for over twenty-five years. He studied for Priesthood at Notre Dame Seminary in New Orleans. His work has been published in the U.S. A, England, and Canada, and he has a collection, Appetite, with Red Hen Press: http://redhen.org/book/?uuid=26010B90-F50B-AE04-1A31-0B86BB199EA4 and a collection Angels & Visitors for publication in 2022 with Wipf & Stock Publishing.
