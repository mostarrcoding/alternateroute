[Alternate Route]

Welcome to [Alternate Route]. We publish online quarterly and are open to submissions year-round to writers globally. Our publication dates are end of January, April, July, and October. Thank you to the respective self-publishing services which we utilize.

Submission Guidelines

Send us a submission which contains up to 5 pages of creative writing and/or art. Please limit writing to no more than approximately 2000 words & poetry: no more than 5 poems. Email your submission to mike [at] alternateroute [dot] org with your name and a 3rd person biography.

Upon acceptance of your work to our zine via email response, we request exclusive electronic first worldwide serial rights, which are returned to the author upon publication. Unless otherwise requested, the font of your piece will be changed to fit the journal.
