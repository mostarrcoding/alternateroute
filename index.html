[Alternate Route]

Welcome to [Alternate Route]. We are an indie literary journal for the outcast. Join us! We publish online quarterly and are open to submissions year-round to writers globally. Our exact online publication dates are January 31, April 30, July 31, and October 31. We strive to release paperback and eBook versions of our zines as close to the online publication dates as possible. Thank you to the respective self-publishing services which we utilize.

Issue 7 (Summer 2022) is out!

In light of the ongoing environmental crisis of global warming and the detrimental decision by the U.S. supreme court to slash the constitutional right to an abortion, Issue 8 will be themed "Mother Earth". Please send us your writing and art that speak on global warming, environmentalism, conservation, ecology, and/or the right to an abortion, equal wages for equal work, spousal abuse, workplace abuse, etc.

Submission Guidelines

Email up to 5 pages of creative writing and/or art (poetry, prose, short stories, art, photography, etc.) to mike [at] alternateroute [dot] org with the following information included in the body of the email:

  • Your name as you would like it to appear in the zine
  • A 3rd person biography, short and sweet does it (typically includes previous published work, author geographical location, but may include whatever you choose about yourself)
  • Optionally, any other things you'd like us to hear (a "cover letter" if you will)

Upon acceptance of your work to our zine via email response, we request exclusive electronic first worldwide serial rights. That means that you must not publish your work elsewhere until it appears online in our zine, after which rights are reverted to you and you can do whatever you want with your work.
