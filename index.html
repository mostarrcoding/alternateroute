[Alternate Route]

Welcome to [Alternate Route], beatnik! We value rebellious, transgressive writing with attitude. Some examples of beat poets include Jack Kerouac, Allen Ginsberg, Anne Waldman, Elise Cowan, Diane di Prima, and William Burroughs.

The submission window for Winter 2023 is now open and will be open until approximately January 31, 2024. Feel free to submit after reading our submission guidelines, link below; and to those here to read, please enjoy the literature. Good luck!
