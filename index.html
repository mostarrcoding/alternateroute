[Alternate Route]

Welcome to [Alternate Route]. We publish online quarterly and are open to submissions year-round to writers globally. Our online publication dates are end of January, April, July, and October, with paperback and eBook out shortly thereafter respectively.

Submission Guidelines

Please follow the below guidelines when submitting material for review and potential publication in our magazine.

  • Up to 5 pages of creative writing and/or art
  • Limit writing to no more than ~2000 words
  • No more than 5 poems
  • .DOC(X) or .ODT format preferred
  • No .PDF (formatting gets messed up during txfer)
  • Paste in body of email acceptable
  • .TXT or .RTF format work, too
  • Email submission to mike [at] alternateroute [dot] org
  • Send your name/penname and a 3rd person biography

We request exclusive electronic first worldwide serial rights, which are returned to the author upon publication.

Unless otherwise requested, the font of your piece will be changed to fit the journal.

We encourage artists & writers who are considering submitting to the journal to first peruse at least one of our prior issues to get a feel for it. Issue links can be found below under "Zines".
