Welcome to [Alternate Route]. We are an indie beatnik literary arts journal for those that wander tha path less traveled. Our doors have reopened and we are once again accepting submissions. Note that publications have switched to twice yearly: April 30th, and October 31. Please send up to 3 poems, 1 short story, or 3 works of art to editor@alternateroute.org.
