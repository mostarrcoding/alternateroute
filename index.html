[Alternate Route]

Welcome to the [Alternate Route] homepage. [Alternate Route] is an indie beatnik lovie-dovie literary e-zine for the outcast traversing the forgotten, the other road. We walk here because it is unpaved. Think on the other side of the fence, forget the disguise, move your senses, engage the silence, hear your voice, exist proudly, love love love, live in that world of fantasy, travel through space and time a hero, discover & heat & familialize yourself with beauty, and know and write the path less traveled.

We publish online quarterly and are open to submissions year-round to writers globally.

Our exact online publication dates are January 31, April 30, July 31, and October 31.

We strive to release paperback and eBook versions of our zines as close to the online publication dates as possible. Thank you to the respective self-publishing services which we utilize.

Submission Guidelines

Email up to 5 pages of creative writing and/or art (poetry, prose, short stories, art, photography, etc.) to mike [at] alternateroute [dot] org with the following information included in the body of the email:

  • Your name as you would like it to appear in the zine
  • A 3rd person biography, short and sweet does it (typically includes previous published work, author geographical location, but may include whatever you choose about yourself)
  • Optionally, any other things you'd like us to hear (a "cover letter" if you will)

Writing in your pieces should be in standard font (something like Times New Roman or Garamond size 10-12).

Note that all formatting in your piece will be overwritten upon publication in our journal (except spacing and indentation), as we use a custom font and formatting. Don't worry; it looks nice :). (For a sample, feel free to peruse one of the previously published issues below).

Also note that our journal uses a 5.5" x 8.5" page size, and some 8.5" x 11.5" pieces with multiple columns or interesting indentation, for example, may not fit. Try adjusting your document's page size to see how you want the formatting to look.

Submit your pieces as separate individual attachments (one attachment per piece) with the title of the piece as the file name. If any pieces are meant to be coupled together, let us know and how you'd like them coupled (e.g. a diptych or triptych of photographs).

Please title all your artwork!

We do not yet offer financial compensation for accepted work, but are working on growing our readership and hope to have sufficient funds to be able to pay out in the future.

We are listed onCLMP, Duotrope, The Short List, and NewPages and are active on Twitter, so we do advocate for our artists. We have a staff of 1 but time permitted and quality of work pending, we may at our discretion submit content in our magazine for prizes and awards.

Upon acceptance of your work to our zine via email response, we request exclusive electronic first worldwide serial rights. That means that you must not publish your work elsewhere until it appears online in our zine, after which rights are reverted to you and you can do whatever you want with your work.

We accept simultaneous submissions, but please let us know as soon as your work is accepted elsewhere if/when it is. Thank you.

We now accept previously published work! Please include the previous publication's location/title/publication information so we may note it in the footnotes of your work upon publication in our journal.

We wholeheartedly welcome creations from downtrodden voices.
