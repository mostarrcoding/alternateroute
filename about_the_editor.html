About the Editor

Michael Starr has been writing poetry since 2004. He has been published in BlazeVOX, Aberration Labyrinth, etc. Full-length works are available online on Amazon and at Barnes and Noble. Lives in California with his family. Web designer, web developer, biologist, engineer, & firm believer in freedom of speech, but still open to good ideas. Feel free to contact editor@alternateroute.org for any questions. Thank you for your time. (Yes, it's painful.)
